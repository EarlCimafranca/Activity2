FSUU

The Father Saturnino Urios University, also referred to by its acronym FSUU, is a private Roman Catholic coeducational basic and higher education institution run by the Diocese of Butuan in Butuan City, Philippines. It offers preschool, elementary, junior & senior high school and college courses.
