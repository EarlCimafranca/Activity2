OPIS

Can I use G-Suite to login in OPIS ?
The answer is No, you can't use your G-suite account to login in your OPIS.
How to access my OPIS
1. Login to http://opisv3.urios.edu.ph/aims/students

2. Login using student number as your username and your family name [ caps lock ] as password.
